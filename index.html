Sample of past projects

Pantheon: AI-facilitated metacognition

Head of technology

Pantheon is an experimental AI-facilitated metacognition tool and research prototype exploring new ways to use LLMs to improve human thinking. Intricate, customizable AI characters and a novel, tree-like UI structure empower the user to think and work in new ways.

Cities of Orare: A vision of the future

Co-author

Cities of Orare paints the picture of a positive future in 2045, where AI-powered prediction markets and digital democracy amplify collective intelligence, enhancing liberal democracy, economic distribution, and policy-making. Cities of Orare won the Foresight Institute Worldbuilding Course of 2024.

Harmony: Multi-user epistemic chatbot

Head of technology

Harmony is a chatbot facilitating users in the double crux technique, enabling smoother argument resolution. It was created as part of the Epistea fellowship program of 2023.
