Hi, I'm Sofi

About

I'm the co-founder and CTO at Mosaic Labs, where I build experimental LLM interfaces to facilitate group deliberation.

I've worked as a full-stack engineer in web, mobile, and game projects since 2017.

Outside work, I live a nomadic lifestyle, and spend my time exploring communities, embodiment, spirituality, and improvisation in art and life.

Current positions

AI Safety Camp

Project highlights

Nexus: AI-mediated deliberation

Technical Lead & System Architect

A platform for group deliberation that uses large language models to help groups understand each other better and surface insights and solutions that wouldn't have been found otherwise.

Autoblog: Dynamic content personalization

Creator & Developer

An interface that helps writers publish authentic thoughts while enabling readers to make sense of them in their unique context. Using AI to dynamically translate and personalize content, Autoblog demonstrates how technology can scale nuance and context in ways previously impossible.

Pantheon: AI-facilitated metacognition

Technical Lead & System Architect

An AI-facilitated metacognition tool exploring new ways to use LLMs to improve human thinking. Intricate, customizable AI characters and a novel, tree-like UI structure empower the user to think and work in new ways.

Cities of Orare: A vision of the future

Co-Author

A vision of a positive future in 2045, where AI-powered prediction markets and digital democracy amplify collective intelligence, enhancing liberal democracy, economic distribution, and policy-making. Cities of Orare won the Foresight Institute Worldbuilding Course of 2024.

Harmony: Multi-user epistemic chatbot

Technical Lead & System Architect

A Slack- and Discord-based chatbot facilitating users in the double crux technique, enabling smoother argument resolution. Created as part of the Epistea fellowship program of 2023.
